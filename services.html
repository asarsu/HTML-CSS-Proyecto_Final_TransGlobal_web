Our Services

Maritim Transport

We offer a wide network of maritime services at strategic ports around the world, ensuring efficient and customized solutions for every import or export operation.

Every country needs to connect its markets to global trade, and at Trans Global LLC we meet that need with world-class services. We handle your cargo with the same care and attention as a first-class passenger, ensuring punctuality, safety, and operational excellence in every shipment.


Inland Trasportation


We offer comprehensive land transport solutions, connecting borders and facilitating the national and international distribution of your goods. Whether by road or rail, we guarantee safety, compliance, and a high operational standard on every journey.


Air Freight


We streamline your operations with fast, reliable air transport services tailored to the demands of international trade. At Trans Global LLC, we work with reputable airlines and manage your air cargo with traceability, efficiency, and personalized attention to and from Bolivia.


Special Projects


At Trans Global LLC, we have the technical and operational expertise to manage the transportation of oversized and specialized cargo from the point of origin to its final destination. Our coverage includes both national and international routes, integrating logistics solutions for sectors such as oil, energy, mining, and general industry. We coordinate each stage of the process with precision and strategic focus, ensuring efficiency and regulatory compliance in complex environments.
