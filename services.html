Our Services

Maritime Transport


We offer an extensive network of maritime services at strategic ports worldwide, guaranteeing efficient and tailored solutions for each import or export operation.

Every country needs to connect its markets to global trade, and at Trans Global SRL, we meet that need with world-class services. We handle your cargo with the same care and attention as a first-class passenger, ensuring punctuality, safety, and operational excellence in every shipment.


Inland Transport


We provide comprehensive land transport solutions, connecting borders and facilitating the national and international distribution of your goods. Whether by road or rail, we ensure safety, compliance, and high operational standards every step of the way.


Air Transport


We streamline your operations with fast, reliable air freight services tailored to the demands of international trade. At Trans Global SRL, we partner with leading airlines and manage your air cargo with traceability, efficiency, and personalized attention to and from Bolivia.


Special Projects


At Trans Global, we offer the technical and operational expertise to manage the transport of oversized and specialized cargo from origin to final destination. Our services cover national and international routes, integrating logistics solutions for sectors such as oil, energy, mining, and general industry. We coordinate each stage with strategic precision, ensuring efficiency and regulatory compliance in complex environments.
