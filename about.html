Leaders in International Logistics from Bolivia

Since 1993, Trans Global SRL has positioned itself as a reliable benchmark in international logistics services from Bolivia. Our company is recognized for offering efficient multimodal transport solutions, tailored to the needs of businesses engaged in global trade.

With more than three decades of experience in the industry, we specialize in air, sea, and land cargo transportation, providing comprehensive support in customs processes, warehousing, and supply chain coordination. Our clients value our punctuality, technical expertise, and commitment to personalized service.

Whether you manage import or export operations, are looking for a trusted logistics company in Bolivia, or need to coordinate complex supply chains, Trans Global provides the infrastructure and knowledge to make it happen—smoothly, safely, and professionally.

Our Core Logistics Services:

  • National and international freight forwarding.
  • Cargo consolidation and deconsolidation.
  • Maritime, land, and air cargo transport.
  • Customs procedures and documentation support.
  • Last-mile and local delivery solutions.
  • Secure warehousing services.
  • Comprehensive logistics project coordination.

Quality Policy

At Trans Global SRL we are firmly committed to the continuous improvement of our services, safeguarding our clients’ interests within the framework of our partners’ guidelines and current regulations.

Our quality policy is aimed at strict compliance with agreements, efficient operational coordination, specialized technical knowledge, and fluid, ongoing communication among all parties involved.

Security Policy

At Trans Global SRL, we view security as a fundamental responsibility in managing both the information and the goods entrusted to us by our clients, service operators, and collaborators.

Security is an integral part of all our logistics operations, and its fulfillment is a shared commitment across our entire organization. Every team member is responsible for ensuring the effective application of security guidelines established by our company, by our partners, and by the relevant national and international authorities.
